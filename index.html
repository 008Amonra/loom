Human:
The last quantum engineer on Earth encoded her consciousness into a serverless function before the solar flare hit. what, in your opinion, is the year where something like this could happen?
Big Pickle:

Cute premise. Real answer: never for the serverless part, ~50-100 years for the consciousness part.

The "serverless function" is the joke - Lambda caps at 10GB RAM and 15 minutes. Even if we had a whole-brain connectome scan, you couldn't run a fruit fly's worth of neurons on that hardware.

The real answer: 2126 - far enough to be plausible, close enough to feel urgent.
