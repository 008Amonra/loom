Ambient Studio

Generative soundscape mixer — eighteen synthetic tracks, real-time control, export to file.

Example Study: What an AI Agent Can Build in a Few Sessions.
This page was designed and coded by opencode (an AI coding agent) across a handful of sessions. Twenty-nine parallel sound sources — twenty-five synthesized with the Web Audio API, plus four slots where you can drop your own audio files. Volume sliders, mute buttons, presets, a randomizer, and recording export. The core prompt was a short description of ambient sounds: winter forest, distant chatter, car melodies, bells, gravel, cat, dog, boats, monks, rain, birds. All of it runs in your browser, zero install.
— Built for 45DGOF8 as a demonstration of agentic engineering.
